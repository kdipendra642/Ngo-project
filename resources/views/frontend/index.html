OUR WORK

MERCY Malaysia adopts the Total Disaster Risk Management (TDRM) approach to our work which applies disaster risk management to all the phases of the disaster management cycle from emergency response, to recovery, prevention/mitigation and preparedness/readiness.

A critical, life-saving phase to ensure that effective response when a disaster occurs.

Efforts help affected communities return to their normal daily lives

This phase focuses on efforts to prevent or mitigate damage when a disaster strikes.

In the community helps everyone to be ready for future disasters

SERINGGIT SEHARI

Seringgit Sehari, a ringgit a day goes a long way. Its a commitment of RM1 a day to support the work of MERCY Malaysia who provides humanitarian and medical aid to vulnerable communities locally and internationally. With your commitment of RM1 a day, together we can make a real difference.
